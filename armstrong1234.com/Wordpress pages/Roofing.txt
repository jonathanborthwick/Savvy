<h2>ENERGY-EFFICIENT, MOISTURE-RESISTANT.</h2>
</div>
<div class="scala body-text">

Foam roofing is the most energy-efficient material available – over 500% more efficient than conventional roofs. It’s the first intelligent roofing technology for flat and low-sloped roofs, combining insulation and roofing into one material. That means no seams or multiple layers – eliminating the expanding and contracting and separating that allows water and microbes to seep in and damage your roof.

Each Armstrong Foam roof is<a href="http://www.armstrong1234.com/roof-process/"> custom installed</a>. It’s sprayed on as a liquid mixture, and within a few seconds expands up to 30 times its original volume into a watertight, seamless solid that custom forms to the existing substrate. Just look at the advantages of an Armstrong Foam roof:
<ul>
	<li>Tough, seamless construction</li>
	<li>Can be sprayed onto almost any surface including irregularly shaped roofs and uneven surfaces</li>
	<li>Tenacious adhesion – demonstrated in the most recent Florida hurricanes</li>
	<li>Moisture resistant – even when the membrane coating is damaged</li>
	<li>Inch-per-inch, the highest energy efficient roofing material available</li>
	<li>Extremely strong – yet ultra lightweight</li>
	<li>Proven longevity classifies it as a sustainable low-slope roofing system</li>
	<li>In most cases requires only periodic recoating for decades of service</li>
</ul>
